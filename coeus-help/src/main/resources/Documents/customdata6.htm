Custom Data

The Custom Data page of the Award document is configured by your institution when additional information beyond what is collected from the “out-of-box” KC product is required for an Award document.  Custom Attribute maintenance documents and reference tables are defined by your institution accordingly to support such information collection requirements.

Expandable, tabbed page sections represent groups made up of custom attributes which are used to define the section that the attributes are displayed within.  These group names are the section labels.
