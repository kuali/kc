Batch Correspondence

 

Batch Correspondence functionality provides a means for institutions to configure automated reminder notifications to go out to researchers to remind them on a timely basis of certain actions they need to take related to their protocols. The Kuali Coeus system provides Batch Correspondence functionality for the following two scenarios.

 

Ø Protocol Renewal Reminders – Batch correspondences related to renewal reminders serve to remind researchers when they need to renew their protocol because their protocol’s expiration date is approaching.

 

Ø Reminder to IRB Notifications – Batch correspondences related to Reminder to IRB notifications remind researchers to revise and re-submit their protocols when it has been returned for SMR (Specific Minor Revisions) or SRR (Substantive Revisions Required)

 

 In addition to generating reminders for the researcher, the Batch Correspondence also allows institutions to set up a final action to be taken on the protocol by the system at the time of the final reminder. The system currently allows the following actions – Close, Abandon, Expire and Suspend.
