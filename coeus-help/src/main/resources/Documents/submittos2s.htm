Submit to S2S:

For most situations, once the Proposal Development document is complete and approved by all parties in workflow the OSP office will perform the s2s Grants.gov submission.  The Submit to S2S button is available to users with the appropriate permission. The OSP office will click this button to electronically transmit the proposal to Grants.gov or Research.gov.

Once electronic submitted, status updates are available on the S2S Opportunity Search screen, Submission Details Tab.
