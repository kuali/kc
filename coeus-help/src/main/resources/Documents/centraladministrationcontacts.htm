Central Administration Contacts

The Central Administration Contacts section displays name, unit, project role, phone and email information about the individuals who will be involved in institutional proposal administration and/or potential subsequent award administration.

Figure 162 Institutional Proposal Document, Contacts Page, Central Administration Contacts Section Example

 

Table 66 Institutional Proposal Document, Contacts Page, Central Administration Contacts Section Column Descriptions

Column

Description

Person

Required.  The name of the central admin contact.  Click the direct inquiry icon or to view the Person document details.

Unit

The name of the unit. Click the direct inquiry icon or to view the Unit document details.

Project Role

Required.  The role of the central admin contact on this project.

Office Phone

The telephone number for the central admin person.

Email

The e-mail address for the central admin contact person.

Actions

Click the add  button to add your selection to as a row in the table below, which will become a numbered line item.  Click the delete button to remove a previously-added line item row from the table.

  For more information about basic line item functionality, see “Common Line Item Operations” in Common E-Doc Operations.
