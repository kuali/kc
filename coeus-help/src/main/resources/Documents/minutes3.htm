Minutes

 

The Minutes section of the Meeting Actions page allows you to generate and view versions of minutes.

Figure 1267 Meeting Document, Meeting Actions Page – Minutes Section

 

Table 513 Meeting Document, Meeting Actions Page – Minutes Section Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Generate Minutes

Click the submit button to generate the minutes document.  The new version is displayed as a new table row in the View Agenda subsection below.

View Minutes

Version

Versions of minutes are automatically tracked and by default, the most recent is displayed first.

Date Created

The date the minutes document was generated.

Actions

Click on send minutes button  to send the meeting minutes to the IACUC committee members. Click on the view button  to open a pdf version of the meeting minutes for viewing.
