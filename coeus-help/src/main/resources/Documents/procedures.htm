Procedures

 

The Procedures page is for capturing details of the procedures that will be performed on each species. The details include personnel involved in performing each procedure, the location where procedure will be performed and specific questions related to the procedure that the institute might have defined. In addition the Procedures page allows the investigator to provide a high-level timeline and overview of all the procedures that will be conducted as part of the research project.

Figure 1107 Protocol Document – Procedures Page Layout

The Procedures page is split into two main sections – one for capturing the Overview and Timelines and the other to capture Procedure details.
