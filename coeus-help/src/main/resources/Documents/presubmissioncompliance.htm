Pre-Submission Compliance

 

The Pre-Submission Compliance group on the Unit menu provides convenient, efficient access to KC functions that are commonly used by Unit users.  It contains “create new” and “lookup existing” icon buttons that take you to new, blank e-docs or lookup screens that allow you to search for e-docs, respectively.

Figure 98 Unit Menu – Pre-Submission Compliance Group

The Pre-Submission Compliance group contains seven functions in two subgroups (Conflict of Interest and Protocols), each with “create new” and “lookup existing” icon buttons.  Each is summarized in the table that follows, along with cross-references to more detailed instructions on how to use the screens they take you to.
