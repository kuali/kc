Overview and Timeline

 

The Overview and Timeline section allows investigators to provide a summary of all procedures that will be performed as part of the research and provide timelines for the procedures.

 

   

  Though the Overview and Timeline is meant to capture a summary and timeline of all procedures to be performed, it’s up to each implementing institution on how they want to utilize it, based on their own business practices.
