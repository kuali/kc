From coeus:

The Proposal Hierarchy tab consists of two panes. The left pane contains a tree that graphically shows all proposals in the hierarchy, and the right pane contains information about the selected proposal. This tab is the same in all proposals in the hierarchy.

Click Show Legend at the bottom of the Proposal Hierarchy tab to see the icons used in the tree.

Click Hide (shown above) to collapse the legend.

In the tree click  (expand) icon next to a child proposal to display all budget versions and the one marked final .

Click  to collapse the tree.

Click a proposal number to display details of that proposal in the right pane.
