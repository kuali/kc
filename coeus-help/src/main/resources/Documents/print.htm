Print

 

Figure 234 Proposal Summary Page> Print sub-panel with Print Grants.gov Forms subsection expanded.

The Print subpanel consists of two subsections: Grants.gov (system-to-system/s2s) forms and sponsor form (paper) packages.  Expand the subsection and select the form(s) and then click [print selected] located in the center of last line in the subsection. The form(s) and any PDF attachment files will open in a separate browser window.
