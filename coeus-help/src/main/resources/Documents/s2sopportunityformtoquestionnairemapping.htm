S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping

The S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping (a.k.a. ‘S2s Form to Questionnaire’) maintenance document is used to identify a questionnaire in KC that is associated with a particular Grants.gov form, so that the form can be mapped to a questionnaire in the pre-award module for use in Proposal Development documents.

The s2s forms maintained here are stand-alone S2S forms not to be confused with the S2S Form Supporting Questionnaire..

Edit a S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping Section

The Edit a S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping section of the S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping  maintenance document is a unique section that allows you to specify and maintain the details associated with the reference table record in fields.

 

Table 722 S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping Maintenance Document - Edit S2S Opportunity Form to Questionnaire Mapping Section Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Opportunity Name Space

Required.  Enter the URL of the form location on the Grants.gov site.

Form Name

Required.  Enter the textual name of the form, as formatted on the Grants.gov site.

Questionnaire Id

Required.  Enter a numeric value to uniquely identify this form in KC.
