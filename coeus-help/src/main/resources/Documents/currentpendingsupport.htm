Current & Pending Support

 

The Current & Pending Support link in the Quicklinks group on the Researcher menu causes the Current & Pending Support screen to display.  This screen allows you to select a Person using a lookup, and then generate either a Current or Pending Report.  The word “Support” refers to people who support a research project.  Two buttons allow you to initiate the report, which retrieves results and displays them in a table format as with any search results in KC, and generate a PDF file you can view, download, and print.  Results include information that identifies the sponsor and award, the role of the person, the award amount, key dates, and effort percentages.  The report can also be exported as is standard in KC.
