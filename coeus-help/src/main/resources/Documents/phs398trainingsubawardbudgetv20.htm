PHS 398 Training Subaward Budget V2.0

KRACOEUS-6666 Release 5.2 S2: Updated attachment form allows 30 subaward attachments (previously only 10).

A completed PDF extracted budget form must be uploaded to the Sub Award Budget panel (Budget Actions tab) of the KC Proposal Development Budget.  Locate and download an appropriate NIH training grant opportunity from the Grants.gov website. Open the Adobe opportunity form set and then click the checkbox beside the PHS 398 Training Subaward Attachment(s) form in the Option section. Click on the form title to jump to the form location or scroll down the Adobe package to the page(s) with the Subaward form. Select the button labeled “Select to Extract…” and save the file to your local computer. Complete the required budget entries – subaward budget requirements are identical to those of the proposal lead budget. Upload the completed Adobe form to your KC Proposal Development budget.

 

 

 

•      The extracted budget file for Subawards must be checked as “Subaward/Consortium”

•      Training Subaward budgets must conform to the same sponsor rules and requirements as the primary applicant budget. Please reference your sponsor’s submission guidelines for specific details. Once the form is completed, the file can be uploaded in the KC Budget Subaward screen.

•      Within the Subaward screen of the Budget, Add a Subaward and search the Organization records to locate their ID, and then upload the completed extracted PDF file. If the form is valid and the dates are compatible (either match, or fall within the start and end dates of your proposal), the funding data will populate to details table, and the line items will be inserted into your prim budget. The uploaded Subaward form will be attached to the Grants.gov Subaward form in your S2S submission if you select to ‘include’ this optional form.
