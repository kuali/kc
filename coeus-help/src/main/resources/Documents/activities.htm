Activities

Figure 222  Negotiation Document > Activities & Attachments Section > Activities Subsection – Sort By Options

After an activity has been added and saved to a Negotiation document, it appears as a hidden subsection under the Activities section.  When you click the show button, you can view the details, including location, description, key dates, and any attachments.
