Budget V1.1 (Budget Attachments)

 

Instructions: Select and save a Grants.gov opportunity containing the Budget V1-1 form in your federally-sponsored proposal.

Navigate to the KC Proposal Attachments panel of the Abstracts and Attachments tab

Select the Narrative Type:  Budget_Attachments (ID 57)

Browse/return attachment:

Ø Search and select to save your narrative document to this narrative type.

Enter a Description. This is required for this Multiple-type narrative.

Ø The entered title should be unique to this upload. The text entered into the KC field should not contain ANY spaces or special characters, as other characters or symbols will cause errors in transmission.

Use only Numbers 0-9

Uppercase  A-Z or lowercase a-z,

Periods (.), dashes (-), and underscores (_)

Save the narrative.

Ø Click the ‘add’ button
