To Add Units:

1.  On the Unit Details tab, click the Lookup/Add Multiple Lines button to open the window.

2.  Enter your search criteria in the Unit Lookup window and then click Search.

3.  In the results window, click the checkbox(es) beside the unit(s) you wish to add and then click return selected button; the unit(s) will be added to the list.

•      If you do not see the unit you desire, click the <Refine Search button to return to the criteria entry window.

•      If you wish to exit the search, click the Close button at the bottom of the screen.
