IACUC Person Training

The IACUC Person Training maintenance document provides a mechanism to the IACUC Administrators to maintain training records for IACUC personnel that can be displayed within the IACUC protocol by clicking on “view training details”  button besides personnel listed within Procedure/Personnel sub-tab.

The IACUC Person Training record links the training a IACUC personnel has attended (recorded within the Person Training maintenance document) to Species and Procedures.

This training information  helps IACUC Administrators and IACUC reviewers to determine whether an IACUC personnel is qualified to perform a procedures on species as documented within the IACUC protocol.
