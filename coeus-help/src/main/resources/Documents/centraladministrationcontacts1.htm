Central Administration Contacts

The Central Administration Contacts section of the Contacts page provides a way for you to view contact information for central administration staff that has been automatically populated based on the specification of the Lead Unit.  It also has a handy way to quickly view complete contact information providing detail beyond what is displayed.

Figure 1325 Award Document, Contacts Page, Central Administration Contacts Section – Example

To view additional Person or Unit details, click the corresponding direct inquiry (open book) icon.  This causes the detailed information e-doc to be displayed in a popup browser window from the Person and Unit maintenance e-docs.
