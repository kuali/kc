Special Review

The Special Review page of the Award document is used to specify details of and review the status of other related institutional projects (like IRB or IACUC Protocols) or groups (like Foundation Relations, or International/Insurance review).  Special Reviews utilizes common line item functionality for selection and entry of information in fields and then gives you the ability to add that information as a numbered line item.  Comment entry is also permitted.  The status update allows you to keep track of special reviews that are important to the administration of the award. IRB and IACUC documents may be linked electronically using system parameters that will link the documents, providing status and date updates to the award document.

(See System Admin > Configuration > Parameters: irb.protocol.award.linking.enabled and iacuc.protocol.award.linking.enabled)
