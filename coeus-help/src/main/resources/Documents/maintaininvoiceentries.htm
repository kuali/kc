Maintain Invoice entries:

To record an invoice, From the Subaward > Financial tab, scroll to the bottom of the page and click the add invoice action button; a new tab will generate in your web browser to enter the details. The document’s status will be “Initiated” until it is saved, and completes the approval workflow or has been blanket approved.  The invoice information will then be populated to the Subaward Financial tab.

The Subaward Invoice document will open in a new tab in your web browser to enter the details. Be  aware that when you close this invoice document, your Subaward document will still be open in the originating browser tab.

 

 

Figure 1528: Subaward Document > Financial Page > Add Invoice action generates the Subaward Invoice page.

The Subaward Invoice page consists of seven (7) panels: Document Overview, Subaward Invoice, Subaward Summary, Subaward Amounts, Notes & Attachments, Ad Hoc Recipients, and Route Log.

 

The Document Overview panel:

The document overview panel the standard section that appears on most e-docs.  It contains one field that is required – Description, which appears in doc search and action list table column cell display.  Some institutions may determine a prescribed value for entering in this field to support searches. Otherwise, enter any value to meet the requirement.

Enter the information from the subcontractor’s invoice in this panel. The attachment field is provided to upload an electronic version of the invoice.

Ad Hoc Recipients

The Ad Hoc Recipients section allows you to interrupt the normal workflow routing of the document and include individuals or workgroups in the routing path. Ad Hoc Routing does not supersede the normal workflow routing of the document but is in addition to the normal routing.

The Ad Hoc Recipients section has two subsections: Person Requests and Ad Hoc Workgroup Requests. Use one or both of the sections to route the document to a person, workgroup, or both.

To complete the initiated Subaward Invoice, the document must be saved then brought to a ‘final’ status by either using the “submit’ button to put the document into workflow for approval routing or using the’ blanket approve’ option.
