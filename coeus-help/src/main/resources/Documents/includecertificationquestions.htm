Include Certification Questions

If proposal persons, such as Key Personnel, have not been defined to require certification, the option is available to add the certification questionnaire by means of the [Include Certification Questions] button.

Click the button to generate the certification questionnaire; click [save] to generate the Notify button. Once added, the questionnaire can be deleted by clicking the [Remove Certifications Questions] button.
