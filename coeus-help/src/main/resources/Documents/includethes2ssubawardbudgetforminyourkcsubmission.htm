Include the S2S Subaward Budget Form in your KC submission

 

1.  Select the Subaward form in the Grants.gov Submission window:

a.  From the Proposal section > S2S tab select your opportunity.

b.  Expand the Forms subpanel and select to “Include” the “Subaward Budget.”

c.  The form selection will be saved when you navigate from this page, or click the Save button at the bottom of the page to add this optional form to be included with this submission.

2.  Locate the opportunity at Grants.gov. (www.grants.gov > Find Grant Opportunities)

3.  Download the Adobe opportunity form set.

4.  Extract the Subaward form and save the file to your desktop.
