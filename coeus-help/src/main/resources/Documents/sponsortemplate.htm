Sponsor Template

The Sponsor Template section is used to apply a sponsor template to an Award document.

Award templates can significantly decrease the amount of time it takes to create an award and significantly increase award accuracy. Templates can include all the standard contact types,  report types & frequencies, and  all the terms and conditions required for a particular Sponsor Award Type (NIH R01’s or Fellowships, FDP awards, a Foundation award, or a commercial sponsor’s general terms).

Once the template is applied, the administrator can edit the items inserted by the template to customize the award to match the sponsor aggrement.

Maintaining templates will improve your reporting accuracy and keep your award administrators happy.

Users with Administrator authorization may add and delete sponsor templates and use the sync to template function.

Sync to Template Function

 applies the information from the selected template to the award.  This may include reporting requirements, payment information, comments, terms, and other information.  For example, if the template lists annual financial reports, KC adds those to the Payment, Reports & Terms tab. 

When you click sync to template, KC asks you some confirmation questions before it makes any changes to the Award.

 

You can use sync to template even if some fields are empty in the existing award.
