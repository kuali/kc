Master Disclosure

 

Figure 787 Reporter > Conflict of Interest > My Disclosures > Master Disclosure navigation path

 

A master disclosure is created when your initial Annual Disclosure or event based or manual disclosures are fully reviewed and approved. The “master” record will contain all your disclosure information.  The Master Disclosure navigation link will open a view-only disclosure report. Reporters must use the Update Master Disclosure to edit their disclosure for future submissions.
