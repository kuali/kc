New Financial Entity

The New Financial Entity page is where the reporter enters details about any new significant financial interests and defines their relationship with that entity.  Reporters must disclose any new SFI’s within a reasonable period of time (generally 90 days) following the date on which the significant Financial Interest is acquired or arises.

In this section, you can disclose new entities and upload relevant attachments. Once the entity is submitted, it can be viewed and updated on the My Financial Entities tab.
