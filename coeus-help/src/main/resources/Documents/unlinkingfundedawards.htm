Unlinking Funded Awards:

Unlinking an IP record used as a Funding Proposal in an Award record is performed from the IP record from the Institutional Proposal Actions Tab > Funded Awards panel.

 

•      To unlink the award, click the box in the Actions column in the row of the award to be unlinked, or click the ‘select all’ option in the bottom of the panel if there is only one item listed.

•      Click the “unlock selected” button located at the bottom of the panel.

•      Respond affirmatively to the confirmation message.

•      If there was only one funded award linked to this IP record, the record’s status will revert to “pending” from “funded.”
