Financial

The Financial section of the Institutional Proposal page contains information about the project costs for each budget period.

The information in the Financial section prepopulates from the source Proposal Development Budget document > Budget Summary panel.  You may enter the information directly in the Financial section for proposals that were not routed through the Proposal Development and Budget module
