Agenda

 

The Agenda section of the Meeting Actions page allows you to generate a meeting agenda document.  Subsequent modifications are permitted, and historical versioning is tracked and viewable.

Table 358 Meeting Document, Meeting Actions Page – Agenda Section Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Generate Agenda

Click the submit button to generate the agenda document.  The new version is displayed as a new table row in the View Agenda subsection below.

View Agenda

Version

Versions of agendas are automatically tracked and by default, the most recent is displayed first.

Date Created

The date the agenda was generated.

Actions

Click on send agenda button  to send the agenda to the IRB committee members. Click on the view button  to open a pdf version of the agenda for viewing.
