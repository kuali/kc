Update Master Disclosure

 

Figure 806 Reporter > Conflict of Interest > My Disclosures > Update Master Disclosure navigation path

 

A master disclosure is created when your Annual Disclosure or event based or manual disclosures are fully reviewed and approved. The “master” record will contain all your disclosure information.  The Update Master Disclosure navigation link will open the master where the Reporter may edit their disclosures to update their disclosure information.
