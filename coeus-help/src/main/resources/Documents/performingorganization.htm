Performing Organization

The Performing Organization designates the location(s)/unit(s) where project effort will take place. For some implementations, this information will be the same as the Applicant Organization. For others, like multi-campus implementations, the Performing Organization will be their local campus address. For most proposals, this organization should not be changed.

Click the direct inquiry icon to view the Organization information, or click the lookup icon to search for and return a different organization value.
