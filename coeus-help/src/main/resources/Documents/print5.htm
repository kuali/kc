Print

 

The Print section of the Meeting Actions page allows you to select meeting-related information and print it.

Figure 1268 Meeting Document, Meeting Actions Page – Print Section Example

 

Table 514 Meeting Document, Meeting Actions Page – Print Section Field Descriptions

Field

Description

Roster

Click within the checkbox to select the item.  A check mark appears within the checkbox to indicate the item is selected.  Click within the checkbox again to clear the mark and deselect the item.

Future Scheduled Meetings

Click within the checkbox to select the item.  A check mark appears within the checkbox to indicate the item is selected.  Click within the checkbox again to clear the mark and deselect the item.

Click the print selected button after selecting Roster and/or Future Scheduled Meetings to print to your local printer.
