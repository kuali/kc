Committee

The Committee page of the Disclosure document allows you to:

•      define and maintain COI Committee information (committee name, home unit, review requirements, quorum requirements, members, contact information, roles, permissions)

•      define and maintain COI Committee meeting information (add  a meeting -single or recurring- to a COI Committee schedule, locations, dates, times, status, agendas, minutes, etc.)

Disclosure documents that have been submitted and assigned to COI Committee meetings have the following key information details summarized for committee review:

Disclosure Number
Reporter Name
Research Project Title/Annual Disclosure Title
Disclosure Type (Event-Based – IRB, Proposal, Award – or Annual)
Submission Review Type
Submission Status
Submission Date
Actions (Link to view full disclosure)
