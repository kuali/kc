Commitments

The Commitments tab contains three sections that identify financial commitments for the award.  These may come from sources other than the sponsor.  The Commitments tab includes Cost Sharing, Rates, and Preaward Authorizations sections that provide details about the award.

Table 642 Award Document, Commitments Page – Section Descriptions and Subtopic Links

Section (linked to subtopic)

Brief Description

Cost Sharing

The part of total project costs paid from sources other than the sponsor, as committed in the proposal budget.  This section displays percentage of the total award, date, and amount.

Rates

F&A and Benefits rates applicable to the award.

Preaward Authorizations

Both sponsor-authorized and institutional-authorized amounts committed to the award for the pre-award period, if applicable.
