History

 

Click the calendar icon to select, or type in mm/dd/yyyy format to populate the Beginning On and Ending On fields with the desired dates, then click the filter button to customize the information displayed.  Select the radio button by clicking within a circle to place a dot within it to indicate your selection of the desired node, then click the load button to customize the Summary section so that information displayed in it is restricted to the selected action date range.
