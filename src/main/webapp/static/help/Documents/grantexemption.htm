Grant Exemption

This action allows the IRB Administrator to grant exemption to submitted protocols that have a protocol review type of Exempt. This indicates that the research project does not have to go through an IRB review.

 

Categories of research (primarily educational, behavioral, social science and research involving existing data, medical records, and pathological specimens) that present little or no risk to the research participants can be exempt from IRB Review. Research that qualifies for "Exemption from IRB Review" must meet one of six categories outlined in the Common Rule (e.g. 45 CFR 46.101(b) and should not involve vulnerable research participants except under unique situations.
