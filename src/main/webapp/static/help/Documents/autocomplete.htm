Auto-Complete

KC remembers recent entries for many fields.  KC’s auto-complete feature automatically fills in a field based on entries previously made in the document.  As you begin to type characters that are the same as previous entries, KC displays those previous entries as auto-complete options.  (This is similar in Excel.)  If the auto-complete text matches what you intend to enter in the field, you may select it with your mouse or your arrow keys:
