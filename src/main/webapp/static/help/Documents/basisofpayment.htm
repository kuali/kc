Basis of Payment

Use the Basis of Payment screen to lookup, create, and update Basis of Payment codes. The Basis of Payment is the payment structure for the sponsored award.  For example, the sponsor may pay on a cost reimbursement or fixed price basis. You select a Basis of Payment from the Payment & Invoices panel on the Payment, Reports & Terms tab of the KC Award e-doc.
