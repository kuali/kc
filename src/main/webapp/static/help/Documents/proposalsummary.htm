Proposal Summary

The Proposal Summary tab consolidates information from many other areas, allowing the user to quickly scan the proposal and may eliminate the need to navigate to different tabs. The page displays subpanels of information if the source page is populated with information. Subpanels will appear for Proposal Summary, Budget Summary, Key Personnel (with count), Special Review (with count), Custom Data Information, Questions, Attachments, Validations, and Print. Subpanels will not appear in this page if there is no information on the source page. Users may wish to navigate here to facilitate their review of data during proposal creation, or approvers during their approval review process.
