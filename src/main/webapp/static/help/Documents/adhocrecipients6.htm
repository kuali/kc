Ad Hoc Recipients

The Ad Hoc Recipients section allows you to request review and approval-related actions from individuals or groups.  This generates notifications in the recipients’ action lists for this particular Time & Money document.

This page section does not appear on Award documents with a status of COMPLETED.

 

Figure 626 Time And Money Document Ad Hoc Recipients Section Layout

Ad Hoc Recipients is a standard piece of functionality that appears in multiple e-docs in KC, and is documented in one place.  For more information about how to use this tabbed e-doc section, see “Ad Hoc Recipients” on page 93 in Common E-Doc Sections.
