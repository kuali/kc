Organization Audit Tab

The Organization Audit tab requires the entry of the applicable fiscal year and optional audit information associated with the organization.

 

Figure 576  Organization Audit

 

Table 267  Organization Audit components

Field

Definition

Fiscal Year

Required.  Identifies the institutional fiscal year for which the audit applies.

Audit Accepted

Check the checkbox to indicate the audit has been accepted, otherwise clear.

Audit Comment

Type to enter textual commentary related to the organization audit.

Add

Adds the data you entered in the fields on the Organization Audit tab as an individual record in the database table (displayed as a line item beneath the entry fields?).
