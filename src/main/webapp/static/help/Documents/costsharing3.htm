Cost Sharing

The Cost Sharing section of the Distribution & Income page displays a sum total for each Budget Period.

Figure 709  Award Budget Document > Distribution & Income Page – Cost Sharing Section Example

 

If the parameter ‘budgetCostSharingEnforcementFlag’ is off (where the value is set to “0” to indicate disabled), KC does NOT require that the Total Cost Sharing amount be fully distributed in the current committed Award version.
