Award

The Award page of the Award document includes information about funding proposals, subawards, and the project’s sponsor.

Figure 477 Award Document, Award Page Layout

It contains six expandable, tabbed sections of key information about the Award, including information from the corresponding proposal(s).

Section (linked to subtopic)

Brief Description

General identifying information about the Award document

Funding Proposals

Funding proposal information from linked Proposal or Institutional Proposal documents

Details & Dates

Key information and important dates pertaining to the award

Subawards

Listing of additional award recipient institutions and subaward amounts

Sponsor Template

Tools that allow you to apply a sponsor template to an award and synchronize fields

Keywords

Search for and select applicable science keywords pertaining to the type of research
