Award Actions

The Award Actions page of the Award document has five expandable, tabbed sections that allow for doing things with the document after its preparation has been completed or is nearly complete.  These include sending to others for their review and approval, validating errors in the document, and printing portions of the document you select.

Table 329 Award Document, Award Actions Page – Section Descriptions and Subtopic Links

Section (linked to subtopic)

Brief Description

Data Validation

Check the entire document for errors (related to incomplete information, data issues, & those that would prevent routing and grants.gov submission), view related messages, navigate to areas of document where errors occur to fix them.

Hierarchy Actions

View summarized details of related documents, open related documents, copy related documents, view the hierarchy.

Print

Select portions of the Award document to be included in a generated report suitable for printing.

Route Log

View who is on the route to review and approve the document, the status of the workflow routing , which actions have been or will be performed for the document, and when.
